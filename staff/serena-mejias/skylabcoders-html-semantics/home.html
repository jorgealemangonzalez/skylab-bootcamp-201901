Who we are

SKYLAB is an initiative of a network of professionals with more than 20 years of experience in the online sector. Our Dean, David Monreal has worked for 12 years in InfoJobs with business and product development responsibilities. He has the support of a Board of Counselors made up of professionals with over 25 years in the industry to ensure that our training programs are tailored to the needs of the market.

Know us

Intensity and specialization. Learn in just 11 weeks everything you need to specialize in Javascript and start your career as a web developer.

Experienced teachers. All our teachers are developers who have worked with important technology companies and have a great experience in training.

Focused on the labor market. Our methodology instills you in thinking like a web developer and teaches you software engineering principles in a practical way.
